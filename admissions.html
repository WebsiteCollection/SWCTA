Transportation

We are pleased you are applying to our school. We would like to remind you that according to the address you provide on your application you may or may not fall within our transportation zone.

We are pleased you are applying to our school. We would like to remind you that according to the address you provide on your application you may or may not fall within our transportation zone.

If you are within our transportation zone you will qualify for busing if you live outside the two mile radius from the school.

If you live outside of our transportation and you are selected through the lottery, you will be responsible for providing your own transportation for the full year. CCSD does not allow withdrawal from any magnet school mid-year so please keep that in mind when applying to a school outside of your transportation boundaries and accepting a seat.
