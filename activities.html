Activities

Coyote Congress

Advisor - Mrs. Julian

Coyote Congress is a gathering where students may voice their opinions on current school events and changes they would like to request to take place.

Coyote Congress is run every first Friday of the month in the Cafeteria.

DECA

Advisor - Ms. Tagliaferri

DECA is a career & technical student organization (CTSO) that prepares emerging leaders and entrepreneurs for careers in marketing, finance, hospitality and management in high schools and colleges around the globe.

DECA is scheduled every 2nd and 4th Tuesday in the Ballroom.

FBLA

Advisor - Ms. Nelson

FBLA is a CTSO for student leaders to prepare for careers in business, management, entrepreneurship, and technology. FBLA brings business and education together in a positive working relationship through innovative leadership and career development programs.

FBLA is scheduled every 1st and 3rd Tuesday in Room B112.

FCCLA

Advisor - Mrs. Sirat

FCCLA is a CTSO that provides personal growth, leadership development, and career preparation opportunities for students in Family and Consumer Sciences education.

FCCLA is scheduled every 1st and 3rd Tuesday in the Ballroom.

HOSA

Advisor - Mrs. Smith

HOSA is a CTSO that develops leadership and technical HOSA skill competencies to enhance the delivery of compassionate, quality healthcare by providing opportunities for knowledge of all health science education students, therefore, helping the student meet the needs of the healthcare community.

HOSA is scheduled every Tuesday in the Cafeteria.

Rout(e) 131

Rout(e) 131 is our daily news show that is produced by students in the upper-level video production classes. To become join this program, you have to be a part of Video Production II.
