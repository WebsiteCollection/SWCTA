About

Southwest Career and Technical Academy’s mission is to challenge students to develop knowledge, skills, character, and work ethic in preparation for the 21st century. The school is divided into two smaller learning communities – the Design Academy and the Professional Service Academy. Within those communities, students share English, math, science, social studies, electives, and program classes with fellow peers who share a common career interest. Real-world experiences are simulated and interweaved into the classroom, preparing our students for entry into the world of work, post-secondary training, or college and university life.
