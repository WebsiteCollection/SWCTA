School Zone Law Change

September 2, 2015 by Mr. Statucki

Parents and students,

Please be aware that effective October 1st, Senate Bill 144, Section 22, Subsection 3 goes into effect. This bill states that following:

"The driver of a vehicle shall not make a U-turn in an area designated as a school zone or school crossing zone except:

(a) When there are no children present;

(b) On a day on which school is not in session;

(c) During the period from a half hour after school is no longer in operation to a half hour before school is next in operation;

(d) If the zone is designated by an operational speed limit beacon, during the hours when the pupils of the school are in class and the yellow lights of the speed limit beacon are not flashing in the manner which indicaties that the speed limit is in effect;

(e) If the zone is not designated by an operational speed limit beacon, during the times when the sign designating the school zone or school crossing zone indicates that the speed limit in not in effect."
