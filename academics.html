Infinite Campus

A web based system SWCTA uses that allow students to check grades and help parents more effectively monitor the progress of their child at school. The system provides detailed, real-time information, including: Demographics, Schedules, Assignments, Assessment Scores, Calendar, Grades, Attendance, Graduation Progress, Academic Planner, To Do List, Reports, District and School Notices. Click here to login to Infinite Campus.

Tutoring

Math and Science tutoring will be held every Monday, Tuesday, and Thursday for every week that school is in session. Subject-specific tutoring is offered on specific days. Students are welcome to attend any session. Tutoring is held from 2-4 p.m.

Day of the Week Subject Teacher Room
Monday All Math Courses Ms. Thompson Room E112
Tuesday Algebra II Focus Ms. Ervin Room C106
Tuesday Science Ms. Sager Room B207
Thursday Geometry Focus Mr. Suba Room B214

Tutoring will be cancelled on the following holidays:

  • Nevada Day - October 30, 2015
  • Staff Development Day - November 2-3, 2015
  • Veteran’s Day - November 11, 2015
  • Thanksgiving - November 25-27, 2015
  • Winter Break - December 21-January 1, 2015-16
  • Semester Exam Week - January 11-15, 2016
  • Martin Luther King Jr. Day - January 18, 2016
  • President’s Day - February 15, 2016
  • Spring Break - March 21-25, 2016

The last day of tutoring will be May 21, 2016.
